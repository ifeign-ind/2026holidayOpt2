JW Marriott · Mussoorie 3 nights · 4 days · Dehradun airport base
Day 1 — Arrival
All roads to Mussoorie
Fly into Dehradun · 2 hr drive to JW Marriott
Check in · explore resort · first sundowners
Dinner at JW Café — multi-cuisine buffet
Day 2 — Resort + Kempty
Kids zone, falls & valley views
Morning: 12,000 sq ft entertainment zone — bowling, games, outdoor adventure
Afternoon: Kempty Falls (walkable from resort) · Mussoorie Mall Road & Gun Hill
Evening: Bonfire + karaoke at resort amphitheatre
Day 3 — Leisure + Hills
Trekking, spa & Lal Tibba
Morning: Guided resort trek through walnut groves
Midday: JW Farm Lunch experience · adults: Cedar Spa by L'Occitane

Evening: Putting green · pool time · final dinner
Day 4 — Departure
Leisurely checkout
Late breakfast · resort walk · checkout
Drive to Dehradun airport (2 hrs) · flights home
The Khyber · Gulmarg 3 nights · 4 days · Srinagar airport base
Day 1 — Arrival
Into the mountains
Fly into Srinagar · 2 hr drive to Gulmarg (scenic route via Tangmarg)
Check in · first Himalayan views from balcony
Bonfire dinner · Kashmiri Wazwan introduction
Day 2 — Gondola Day
Ride to 13,000 ft
Morning: Gulmarg Gondola Phase 1 (Kongdoori) — panoramic Himalayan views · all ages
Afternoon: Gulmarg meadow walk · wildflowers in bloom (late May)
Evening: Heated indoor pool · spa · multi-cuisine dining
Day 3 — Leisure + Srinagar
Meadows & Dal Lake
Morning: Resort activities — table tennis, snooker, kids club, spa
Afternoon: Day trip to Srinagar (1.5 hrs) — Shikara ride on Dal Lake · Mughal Gardens
Evening: Return to Khyber · farewell dinner
Day 4 — Departure
Final meadow morning
Morning walk through Gulmarg meadows · breakfast at resort
Drive to Srinagar airport (1.5–2 hrs) · flights home
